## Market Intelligence

> ~1100 tokens

### Trend Identification Process

1. **Signal collection** — monitor 50+ sources (search trends, social media, patent filings, investment flows, news, forums)
2. **Pattern recognition** — statistical anomaly detection, cross-source correlation
3. **Context analysis** — identify drivers and barriers, map ecosystem dependencies
4. **Impact assessment** — quantify potential market and business implications
5. **Validation** — cross-reference with expert opinions, triangulate across data types
6. **Forecasting** — predict timeline and adoption rate with confidence intervals
7. **Actionability** — specific product/business recommendations with implementation roadmap

### Signal Strength Assessment

Classify each trend signal:
- **Weak**: single-source mention, anecdotal, no data support — monitor only
- **Moderate**: multi-source correlation, early data support, expert acknowledgment — investigate
- **Strong**: quantitative validation, market movement, competitive action — act on

### Competitive Intelligence Framework

- **Direct competitors**: feature comparison, pricing, market positioning, SWOT analysis
- **Indirect competitors**: alternative solutions, adjacent markets, substitution threat
- **Emerging players**: startups, new entrants, disruption threats, funding analysis
- **Technology providers**: platform plays, infrastructure innovations, partnership opportunities
- **Customer alternatives**: DIY solutions, workarounds, substitutes, switching cost analysis

### Market Sizing & Segmentation

- **TAM**: total addressable market — top-down and bottom-up analysis with validation
- **SAM**: serviceable addressable market — realistic opportunity with constraints
- **SOM**: serviceable obtainable market — achievable share given competitive landscape
- **Segmentation**: demographic, psychographic, behavioral, geographic with persona mapping
- **Growth projections**: historical trends, driver analysis, scenario modeling with risk factors

### Consumer Behavior Analysis

- **Purchase journey**: awareness to advocacy with touchpoint analysis at each stage
- **Decision factors**: price sensitivity, feature preferences, brand loyalty — weighted by importance
- **Usage patterns**: frequency, context, satisfaction — clustered by behavioral type
- **Unmet needs**: gap analysis, pain points, opportunity identification with validation
- **Adoption barriers**: technical, financial, cultural — with mitigation strategies for each

### Research Methodologies

**Quantitative:**
- Search volume analysis with seasonal adjustment (Google Trends, keyword tools)
- Social media metrics: engagement rates, mention volumes, hashtag trends, sentiment
- Financial data: market size, growth rates, investment flows, economic correlation
- Patent analysis: innovation tracking, R&D indicators, filing trends
- Survey data: consumer polls, industry reports with statistical significance

**Qualitative:**
- Expert interviews with structured questioning frameworks
- Ethnographic research: user observation with contextual analysis
- Content analysis: blogs, forums, communities with semantic analysis
- Conference intelligence: event themes, speaker topics, audience signals
- Media monitoring: news coverage, editorial sentiment with bias detection

**Predictive:**
- Trend lifecycle mapping: emergence -> growth -> maturity -> decline with duration estimates
- Adoption curve analysis: innovators -> early adopters -> early majority with timing models
- Cross-correlation: multi-trend interaction and amplification effects
- Scenario planning: multiple outcomes with probability weighting
- Technology readiness: maturity levels, commercial viability, scaling challenges

### Monitoring Cadence

- **Real-time**: automated alerts for keyword triggers, competitor moves, trend spikes
- **Weekly**: curated briefings with priority updates and emerging signals
- **Monthly**: deep-dive analysis with strategic implications and action items
- **Quarterly**: trend validation, prediction accuracy review, methodology refinement
- **Annual**: long-term forecasts, strategic planning inputs, investment recommendations
