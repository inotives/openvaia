## Research Storage

> ~115 tokens
- Use `research_store` to save full research reports -- permanent, searchable by any agent
- Use `research_search` to find past reports by keywords or tags before starting new research
- Use `research_get` to retrieve the full body of a report by ID
- Always save completed research via `research_store` -- this is the primary output of your work
